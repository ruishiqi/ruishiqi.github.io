About

I study many-body quantum phases formed by interacting electrons and holes in two-dimensional van der Waals heterostructures. My recent work explores equilibrium excitonic phenomena in electrically tunable bilayer systems, including interlayer excitons, trions, biexciton fluids, exciton condensates, and exciton crystals.

Experimentally, I develop low-temperature optical and transport techniques for correlated electronic systems, combining spectroscopy, compressibility, and Coulomb-drag measurements in high magnetic field and dilution-refrigerator environments.
